About Shakan Global Worship

Our Vision, Mission, and Core Pillars

Vision Statement

"To awaken, equip, and release a generation of worshipers who carry the manifest Presence of God, transforming nations through authentic, Spirit-led worship."

Our Mission

Four core commitments guiding our work worldwide

🎓

Train & Empower

We equip worship leaders, singers, and creatives with world-class vocal training, spiritual formation, and leadership development to unlock their full potential.

🎤

Transform Through Events

We host transformative conferences, worship nights, and masterclasses that ignite revival, build community, and deepen encounters with God's presence.

✨

Global Network

We build a global network united by purity, excellence, and Kingdom purpose—connecting worshipers across cultures and continents.

📖

Restore Biblical Foundations

We restore biblical foundations of worship, teaching the heart, theology, and practice of authentic Spirit-led worship according to Scripture.

Our Six Core Pillars

The foundational principles anchoring our movement

🔥 Presence

Cultivating authentic encounters with God's manifest presence. We prioritize creating environments where the Spirit moves freely, believers experience His glory, and testimonies of transformation overflow.

✨ Purity

Upholding holiness in worship and lifestyle. We commit to integrity, moral excellence, and living out the message we sing—modeling Christ's character to a watching world.

⚡ Power

Operating in the Spirit's gifts and anointing with authority and confidence. We teach believers to move in God's power, not their own talent alone, for maximum Kingdom impact.

🎯 Purpose

Worshiping with clear Kingdom vision and divine assignment. Every worshiper's gifts serve God's purposes—we align hearts with His strategic plans for nations and generations.

🌍 Cultural Expression

Honoring all cultures in worship expression and celebration. We celebrate the diversity of God's kingdom—African rhythms, Asian melodies, Western arrangements, indigenous traditions—all worshiping one God.

🤝 Partnership

Building global connections and collaboration across borders. We partner with churches, ministries, and worship movements worldwide to multiply impact and strengthen the global body of Christ.

Our Strategic Timeline

Unfolding God's vision across three strategic seasons

📅 Phase 1: Short-Term (1–3 Years)

Vision: Establish foundational training, build community infrastructure, and reach initial audience.

  • Develop and launch comprehensive vocal training curriculum
  • Host monthly Zoom worship nights and online events
  • Build community platform connecting worshipers globally
  • Create free educational resources and tutorials
  • Recruit and train 50+ volunteer facilitators
  • Reach 500+ active engaged community members
  • Establish social media presence across major platforms
  • Produce 24+ training videos and content pieces

📊 Phase 2: Mid-Term (3–7 Years)

Vision: Scale operations, establish physical presence, and become a recognized global ministry.

  • Launch annual Shakan Global Worship Conference
  • Establish regional ministry hubs in 3–5 continents
  • Produce original worship music and recordings
  • Develop certification programs for worship leaders
  • Host quarterly advanced masterclasses
  • Build partnership network with 30+ churches and ministries
  • Reach 5,000+ active members across regions
  • Publish worship resources, books, and curricula
  • Launch broadcast/podcast reaching millions

🌟 Phase 3: Long-Term (7–20+ Years)

Vision: Establish lasting legacy transforming nations through worship succession and systemic impact.

  • Establish permanent Shakan worship centers in major cities worldwide
  • Train and deploy 1,000+ certified worship leaders
  • Create multi-generational worship movement spanning decades
  • Influence national and international worship culture
  • Build endowment supporting perpetual ministry
  • Reach 50,000+ active members and partners
  • Document and preserve movement history for future generations
  • See measurable transformation in nations through worship and prayer

"I desire Shakan Global Worship to move from generation to generation,
carrying the fire of God's presence with no end — growing, enlarging,
and impacting nations long into the future."
